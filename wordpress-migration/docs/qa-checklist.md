# QA Checklist

## Visual

- Desktop homepage matches snapshot hero, header, product grid, CTA, newsletter, and footer.
- Mobile homepage uses the same content order and readable spacing.
- Product archive renders five-up on wide desktop and collapses cleanly on tablet/mobile.
- Product cards keep stable image ratios and button heights.
- PDP sticky add-to-cart appears only after the main purchase area scrolls away.
- Lab Results cards and search match the current information hierarchy.

## Commerce

- Add-to-cart works for simple products.
- Variant selection updates price, image, dosage label, and add-to-cart variant.
- Volume tier buttons update quantity.
- Checkout accepts payment gateway.
- Shipping threshold matches the current free-shipping rule.
- Taxes are correct for target regions.
- Order emails render with compliant language.

## Data

- Product slugs match Shopify handles where possible.
- Product images are imported and assigned.
- Product technical fields are populated.
- COA batches link to the correct products and variants.
- Latest/current COA is correct per product.
- COA files open publicly.
- Lab Results aggregate counts and average purity match imported records.

## SEO

- Every old Shopify product URL redirects.
- Every old Shopify page URL redirects.
- Policy URLs redirect or preserve equivalent slugs.
- Sitemap generates after launch.
- Analytics and pixels fire once, not twice.

## Compliance

- Access/TOS gate appears before commerce pages if still required.
- Footer disclaimer appears globally.
- Product pages include research-use-only language.
- FAQ and legal pages are reviewed before launch.
